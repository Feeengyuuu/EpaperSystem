# LiveRadar top live cards show avatars

Logged: 2026-05-28T15:54:00-07:00

For LiveRadar on the e-paper display, live streamer cards should show a circular streamer avatar in the lower-left text area. Keep the live screenshot as the full top image, place the avatar below it, and shift the title/subtitle/meta text to the right so it does not overlap.

Avoid adding a second favorite badge on this avatar because the large card already has the favorite icon in the top-right control cluster.
