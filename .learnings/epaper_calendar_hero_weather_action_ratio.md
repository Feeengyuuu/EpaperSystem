# Epaper calendar hero weather action ratio

## [LRN-20260529-113] design

**Logged**: 2026-05-29T00:00:00-07:00
**Priority**: medium
**Status**: active
**Area**: epaper

### Summary
Comic hero weather-background sets should mix weather-control actions with passive hero scenes instead of making every image show a hero creating the weather.

### Details
The user corrected the local top-weather expansion: "not every image needs to create/shape the weather; random few are enough." After generating the first local variants with too many weather-control actions, passive replacements were added for patrol, observation, gliding, hiking, and rest scenes.

### Suggested Action
For future weather-background batches, target roughly 2-3 weather-control images per 7 variants and make the rest passive atmosphere/action scenes. Preserve the same constraints: no baked calendar text/digits, light central date field, original non-IP characters, and comic palette.
