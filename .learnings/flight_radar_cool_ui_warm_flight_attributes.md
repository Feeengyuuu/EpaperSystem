For FlightRadar/SkyRadar, the preferred palette split is a cool blue UI shell
with warm, high-contrast flight attribute colors. Keep `bg`, `panel`, and
`panel2` in blue/teal tones, but use warm yellow/orange/red/brown for
`plane_low`, `plane_cruise`, `plane_high`, `plane_ground`, the right-card
attribute stripes, and the bottom LOW/CRUISE/HIGH/GND legend.
